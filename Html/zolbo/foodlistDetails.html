Shrimp and Broccoli - Fast & Easy Dinner

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Add broccoli and soy sauce, stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and sesame oil, if using.
  6. Serve hot.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 250

Protein: 20g

Fat: 10g

Carbohydrates: 15g

Fiber: 2g

Sugar: 1g

Tips

You can use frozen shrimp for convenience. Just thaw before cooking.
